What is Teacher Talk?

Teacher Talk is an app developed to empower education by streamlining the process of communication between teachers and parents.

Why is this useful?

Teacher Talk saves time by managing contacts, providing professional message templates, and automatically creating a log of your communication. Teacher Talk allows teachers to be more proactive in their communication by simplifying the process from start to finish.

How does it work?

Teacher talk is easy to use. It allows parents to sign up and manage their best contact information. Teachers are present with a familiar email-like view where they can simply select students, their message and hit send. Teacher talk takes care of the rest

Can I make my own message template?

Yes! While Teacher Talk comes with a variety of useful message templates it is easy to add your own message templates at any time
